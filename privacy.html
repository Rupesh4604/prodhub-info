Privacy Policy for ProdHub

Last Updated: July 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ProdHub ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our web application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

User-Provided Information: We collect and store the data you enter into the application, including project details, tasks, and habits. This information is stored securely in your personal Firebase account.

Google Calendar Data: If you choose to sync your Google Calendar, our application requests read-only access to your calendar events. We use this access solely to display your upcoming events within the application's schedule view. We do not store, save, or share your calendar events on our servers. The access is temporary and used only while you are actively viewing the schedule.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the Service.
  • To display your project, task, and habit data to you.
  • To display your Google Calendar events to you within the schedule view.

3. Data Storage and Security

All your project, task, and habit data is stored in your personal Firebase Firestore database, secured by Firebase's standard security rules. We do not have access to this data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any of your personal information or Google Calendar data with any third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
